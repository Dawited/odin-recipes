This is more aguide on how to make your own pizza dough more than pizza but this is more important the stps are simple and easy and dont require to many ingredients and your just one step closer to eating a delicious pizza

Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water.
  • 1 package of dry yest
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees while you prepare your ingredients
  2. Place warm water in bowl, add yeast and sugar, mix and let stand for 10 minutes (until creamy)
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mix and beat till smooth
  4. Let Rest 5 Minutes
  5. Roll dough out and transfrer to a pan
  6. Top with your chosen toppings and bake for 15 to 20 mintues
  7. ENJOY!!!
